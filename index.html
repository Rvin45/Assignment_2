1. Public Transport Role

Public transport is the lifeline of a growing city - shaping how people move, work, and connect. In Melbourne, Metro Trains plays a central role in keeping the city moving, with millions of daily commuters depending on it for accessibility and sustainability. In the 2023-24 financial year, Metro Trains recorded over 182 million passenger boardings, a massive number to say the least, to put it to scale, MCG can hold up to 100,000 people, multiply that by 180. This scale of movement reflects not just the city's reliance on rail transport, but its contribution to easing congestion, reducing emissions, and connecting communities across metropolitan Melbourne.
